Welcome to eVSP

Vidhi Sahitya Prakashan, established in 1968, has published around 15,000 Hindi translations of judgments from the Supreme Court and various High Courts of India. It also publishes legal literature in Hindi authored by renowned jurists. Supporting the Government of India's policy to promote Hindi in legal fields, the organization aims to make legal texts more accessible to judges, lawyers, bureaucrats, law students, and litigants. It contributes to national unity through the spread of Hindi and promotes its use by publishing authoritative Hindi judgments and organizing legal literature exhibitions across India.
